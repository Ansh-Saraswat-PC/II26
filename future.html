v2.0 Roadmap

Beyond the Hackathon.

AirWatch Ward is built with an architecture designed to scale. Our post-hackathon trajectory transforms this prototype from a localized dashboard into a national urban utility.

Accessibility

Omnichannel Push Notifications

Not every citizen will proactively check a web dashboard. V2.0 integrates WhatsApp Business API and SMS gateways to push Gemini-generated, localized health advisories directly to residents' phones when their specific ward crosses critical thresholds.

Multi-City Expansion

Scaling beyond Delhi's 272 wards. Because we rely on Haversine mathematical snapping and open APIs, expanding to Mumbai, Kanpur, or Patna requires only the integration of new municipal GeoJSON files.

Production IoT Integration

Transitioning from simulated fallback data and third-party APIs to a proprietary network of low-cost, production-grade IoT sensors deployed directly on municipal infrastructure (e.g., streetlights).

Data Science

Long-Term Trend Analytics

Building a persistent backend architecture to store multi-month views. This will allow researchers to track seasonal events (like crop burning or Diwali) and correlate our AI's source attributions with respiratory hospital admission rates over time.
